# Phase 24 SEO Keyword Research

> Data collected via DataForSEO API on January 2026
> Location: United States | Language: English
> **Target Market: Orange County, California**

## Executive Summary

**Critical Insight**: "medspa deals" has extremely low search volume (10/month). Users search for **specific treatment costs**, not generic "deals" terminology. SEO strategy should prioritize cost/pricing keywords.

**Orange County Insight**: Newport Beach has the strongest search demand in the region, with "medspa newport beach" at 1,000/month. Regional "orange county" keywords perform well as umbrella terms.

## SEO Recommendations for Orange County

### Title Tag Patterns (Optimized for OC)

**Homepage:**
```
Compare MedSpa Prices in Orange County | Botox & Lip Filler Deals | CostFinders
```

**Region Page (Orange County):**
```
Orange County MedSpa Deals | Compare Botox & Lip Filler Prices | CostFinders
```

**City Pages - Tier 1:**
```
Newport Beach MedSpa Deals | Botox & Lip Filler Prices | CostFinders
Irvine MedSpa Deals | Compare Botox & Treatment Prices | CostFinders
```

**City Pages - Tier 2/3:**
```
{City} MedSpa Deals | Compare Treatment Prices | CostFinders
```

### Meta Description Patterns

**Homepage:**
```
Compare medspa prices in Orange County. Find the best deals on Botox, lip fillers, and skincare treatments in Newport Beach, Irvine & nearby cities. Save 20-70%.
```

**City Pages:**
```
Compare Botox and medspa prices in {City}. Find verified deals on lip fillers, facials & skincare treatments. {X} deals available near you.
```

### URL Structure

Recommended structure for OC market:
```
/ca/orange-county/              - Region landing page
/ca/orange-county/newport-beach/ - Tier 1 city
/ca/orange-county/irvine/        - Tier 1 city
/ca/orange-county/costa-mesa/    - Tier 2 city
/ca/orange-county/[city]/        - All other cities
```
